Led a team at ALX to simulate the development of a transportation solution app, gaining valuable experience in teamwork, project management, and problem-solving skills.

Acting as the the team's UX Researcher and UI/UX Designer, I carefully designed questionnaires for user interviews aimed at understanding user behaviors, needs and preferences. I gathered insights and designed the user interface to be aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly, while also considering the user experience to ensure seamless interactions and intuitive navigation.

Technologies: Miro, Google Workspace, YouTube, Loom
